Just a Boring Description!

"Join us for an unforgettable event of comedic brilliance where every punchline hits the mark. Our stand-up comedy event is your ticket to a world of satire, absurdity, and uproarious laughter. Whether you're a fan of observational humor or love a good dose of wit, this event guarantees endless giggles, chuckles, and aching cheeks from grinning ear to ear. Grab your seat and get ready to LOL with us!"

General Event Rules

  1. It is an Individual competition.
  2. Participants have to prepare a prewritten act. There are no limitations to the topics that can be chosen by the participants.
  3. Language used must be preferably Tamil or English, to cater to the needs of the diverse audience.
  4. The participants can take 5-10 minutes for their performance. Exceeding the time limit will result in negative marking.
  5. The participants must bring their own props, if required.
  6. The participants will be judged on the basis of content, fluency, spontaneity, presentation and sense of humour.
  7. Participants should conform to reasonable standards of decency, vulgar, obscene or offensive language is prohibited.
