Music Fest: Being The Beat

Just a Boring Description!

"Unleash your inner diva or rockstar and be part of the audience that makes this singing event unforgettable. Feel the rhythm, embrace the emotions, and let the songs transport you to a world of pure sonic bliss. Get ready to cheer, applaud, and sing along because, at this event, the stage belongs to the stars, and the magic is in the music!"

General Event Rules

  1. The participants can perform solo or in a team of maximum 4 members.
  2. Maximum duration for a performance should not exceed 6 minutes.
  3. Choice of songs is open to the participants. The participants should not convey any indecent gestures.
  4. The participants are requested to bring pen drive for any background score. The participants are requested to submit the pen drive before the event.
  5. Avoid excessively revealing or offensive attire, to ensure a respectful and enjoyable atmosphere for all.
  6. The participants will be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formation, synchronization, expressions and overall effects.
  7. Bring your own instruments and no further will be provided.
  8. Take care of your belongings. In case of any theft or damage, the organization is not responsible.
