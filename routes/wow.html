Wonder From Waste: Wonder - to - Wow

Just a Boring Description!

The three 'Rs' stand for: Reduce, Reuse and Recycle. They are a part of the waste hierarchy which is used to protect the environment and conserve resources through a priority approach. The aim is to get maximum practical benefits from products and to generate the minimum amount of waste.

The creative art and craft ideas by making: a teapot and a cup, hat, paper vase, wall hangings, greeting cards, Utility box, toys and paper jewelry and many more. The creativity displayed by the children was mesmerizing. The competition promoted artistic fervour in our leaders of tomorrow.

General Event Rules

  1. Team shall consist of 2 members only.
  2. Participants will be given 45 minutes to show their creativity and 2 minutes to talk about the same.
  3. The required waste material and stationary like scissor, thread, etc should be brought by the participants.
  4. Waste material could be anything like tetra packs,bottle , newspaper ,old utensils, jute material etc..
  5. The material would be rejected if not found to be a waste product.
  6. No ready or semi- finished model or matter would be accepted from participant in competition.
  7. Participants will be judged on creativity, utilization of resources, artistic composition and design.
  8. No mobile or Internet means would be allowed to use at the time of Competition.
  9. The decision of the judges will be considered final
