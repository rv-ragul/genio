Techincal Quiz: Tech Spirit

Just a Boring Description!

A technical quiz is a type of quiz that focuses on testing knowledge and understanding of technical subjects, such as science, technology and engineering fields. It may include questions related to concepts, theories and problem-solving techniques.

Technical quizzes may be used to assess the knowledge of students, professionals, or enthusiasts in a particular field, and may be conducted in various formats, such as online quizzes, written tests, or live competitions. The term technical quiz is a capacious one: it can refer to a single game consisting of just a few questions, or it can refer to a large-scale event involving dozens or hundreds of people.

General Event Rules

    Participation Guidelines:
  1. Teams can consist of up to 2 members, Individual participants are also allowed to compete.
  2. Each team is permitted to submit a single entry. Additionally, only one team member is allowed to use a PC or laptop during the competition.
    Competition Format:
  1. Round 1: The Time Warp Participants will be presented with 25 questions to be answered within a time limit of 25 minutes.
  2. Round 2: The Quantum Sprint In the second round, participants will face 25 questions with a reduced time limit of 15 minutes for answering.
    Tiebreaker Rules:
  1. If two or more teams have the same total points at the end of the quiz, the timing of their answers will determine the winner.
  2. The team with the fastest combined response time across all questions will be declared the winner.
    Scoring System:
  1. Teams will earn points for correct answers. Incorrect answers do not result in negative points.
  2. Participants cannot answer questions after the specified time limit has expired
    Fair Play:
  1. Teams are expected to maintain the highest level of fair play and ethical conduct.
  2. Any form of cheating, use of unauthorized materials, or disruptive behavior will result in immediate disqualification.
    Jury Decision:
  1. The decisions made by the jury are final and binding.
  2. Any disputes or discrepancies will be resolved by the jury, whose decision will be conclusive.
    Prize Distribution:
  1. The top two teams or individuals with the highest scores will be announced and awarded.
