Dive into the world of anime with "Anime Enigma" . This non-technical event celebrates the art, stories, and culture of anime.Test your knowledge of iconic anime series and movies in three exciting categories: Big Three, Top Hits, and Anime Movies.

General Event Rules

    TEAM COMPOSITION:
  1. Teams can consist of maximum of 3 members and minimum 2.
    FORMAT:
  1. The contest will consist of multiple choice questions,true/false questions,and picture- based questions related to the Anime World.
    CONDUCT:
  1. Questions will be displayed on the Projector and the particpants had to answer the above through KAHOOT using thier respective Mobile/Laptop.
    1. Total of 40 questions.
    2. it comprises of three categories of questions:
      BIG THREE : which includes ONE PIIECE, NARUTO, BLEACH
      TOP HITS : which includes DEMON SLAYER, ATTACK ON TITANS, DEATH NOTE, HAIKYU,CLASSROOM OF ELITES.
      ANIME MOVIES: Which includes WEATHERING WITH YOU, YOUR NAME, SUZUME, SILENT VOICE, I WANT TO EAT YOUR PANCREAS.
    3. Big three category consists of 5 questions per anime
    4. Top Hits category consists of 3 questions per anime
    5. Anime Movies category consists of 2 questions per anime.
    SCORING:
  1. 1 Point will be awarded for each correct answers and points won't be deducted for wrong answers.
    TIME LIMIT :
  1. 20 seconds will be given for each questions.
    USE OF ELECTRONIC DEVICES :
  1. Unfair use of the eletronic devices / Malpractice  leads to disqualification.
    TIE BREAKER:
  1. In the event of a tie, a sudden tiebreaker questions will be asked to determine the winner.
