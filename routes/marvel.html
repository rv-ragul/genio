General Event Rules

    TEAM COMPOSITION:
  1. Teams can consist of maximum of 3 members and minimum 1.
    FORMAT:
  1. The contest will consist of multiple choice questions,true/false questions,and picture- based questions related to the Marvel Universe.

  1. Questions will be displayed on the screen

    SCORING:
  1. 1 Point will be awarded for each correct answers and points won't be deducted for wrong answers .
    TIME LIMIT :
  1. 15 seconds will be given for each questions.
    USE OF ELECTRONIC DEVICES :
  1. Teams found using such devices will be disqualified.

  1. In the event of a tie , a sudden tiebreaker questions will be asked to determine the winner.
