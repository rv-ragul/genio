General Event Rules

  1. Maximum 4 members in each team are allowed.
  2. Each team will be allotted a particular number.
  3. The clues are to be found in a particular order. A team cannot skip a clue. Skipping a clue leads to disqualification of the team.
  4. An entire team must stay together. It cannot split up to find different clues.
  5. All the means used by the teams should be fair, in case of any violation of the rules or any use of unfair means, teams will be disqualified from the contest.
  6. The Treasure Hunt consists of clues, each one leading to the next clue. Each correctly solved clue carries 10 points. And final Treasure carries 50 points.
  7. All the clues of the teams are at different and located at different places but the final destination i.e. the treasure is same. The team which finds the treasure first wins.
  8. Organizers are free to make any changes which will be informed prior to the competition.
  9. One Winner and One Runner will be awarded.
