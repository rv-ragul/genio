Paper Presentation: PAGE TO STAGE

Just a Boring Description!

Paper presentation is an individual competition where each participant is required to create a paper (essay) in English about a given theme and present it with a presentation (PowerPoint) in front of the judges. The presentation must be interesting, creative and deliver the participants’ ideas within the time limit determined.

The purposes of this competition are to enhance participants’ ability to create papers in English, as well to develop the participants’ ability, creativity to make, deliver their presentations, to build the confidence and courage to deliver ideas to the public.

General Event Rules

  1. Maximum number of participants in a team is 3.
  2. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
  3. The topics can be chosen by your own. It should be focused on one particular topic.
  4. Paper should not exceed 10 pages.
  5. Paper should be in IEEE format. Paper must contain index, list of figures, list of tables, abstract, introduction, point wise description of subjects and conclusion, result and references.
  6. In the same order as mentioned above Each team will be alloted 10 minutes for the presentation and followed by Question and Answer session.
  7. Bring the hardcopy of your paper presentation and softcopy on pen-drive.
  8. Your softcopy of the presentation should be uploaded 2 days prior to the day of presentation.

Note

  1. In case of Plagiarism found in Paper submitted
  2. Team will be disqualified
  3. Formal Dress code is recommended for participants
  4. Participants should carry their respective college ID and ID card is mandatory
