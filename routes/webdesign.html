The three 'Rs' stand for: Reduce, Reuse and Recycle. They are a part of the waste hierarchy which is used to protect the environment and conserve resources through a priority approach. The aim is to get maximum practical benefits from products and to generate the minimum amount of waste.

General Event Rules

  1. Each participant will have 1 hour to complete the task.
  2. Participants must build a dynamic web application from the given set of themes or topics(On-spot) .
  3. The participants are allowed to choose a framework for this round but it must be from the following list of frameworks:
  4. Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, Angular, Vannila-framework, Bulma, Materialize CSS

  5. Note:
  6. Team Size: Participants can compete individually or in a team of two, as per their preference.
  7. Equipment: Participants should bring their own laptops or devices with necessary software and development tools pre-installed.
  8. Resources: Participants can use official documentation, tutorials, and online resources specific to the chosen framework.
  9. Judging Criteria: Judging will be based on functionality, user experience, code quality, and creativity in utilizing the chosen framework's features.
  10. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
