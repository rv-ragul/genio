General Event Rules

  1. We'll be determining the results winner using a method called FIFO, which stands for First-In, First-Out.
  2. When you submit your coding challenge, we'll apply the FIFO method.
  3. If someone gets full marks or if multiple participants score the same, the winner will be chosen based on who submitted their solution first.
  4. If there are no full marks or ties, the winner will be the one with the highest score.
  5. So, make sure to submit your solutions on time and aim for the best result!.
