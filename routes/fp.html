Just a Boring Description!

"Step into the spotlight and witness the runway transform into a canvas of style. Our fashion show event promises an extravagant display of creativity, elegance, and trendsetting designs. General Event Rules

  1. There will be a single round.
  2. The participants should register prior the competition.
  3. Theme would be of your choice.
  4. No props and costumes will be provided by us. Participants have to bring their own costumes and props.
  5. Avoid excessively revealing or offensive attire, to ensure a respectful and enjoyable atmosphere for all.
  6. Vulgarity of any kind would lead to disqualification of the participant from the event.
  7. It is imperative that all participants must possess a valid college ID which must be presented at the time of registration.
