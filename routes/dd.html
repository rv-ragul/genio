"Get ready to dance the night away at our electrifying college dance event! Join us under the shimmering lights, where the beats are contagious, the moves are unstoppable, and the memories are unforgettable. Grab your dancing shoes and let's make this night one for the books!"

General Event Rules

  1. Each team may have 1-8 participants, including coordinators.
  2. Each team will be given maximum of 4 minutes.
  3. Choice of songs is open to the participants. The participants should not convey any indecent gestures.
  4. The participants are requested to bring pen drive to avoid technical disturbance. The participants are requested to submit the pen drive before the event.
  5. No props will be provided by the coordinators. Participants can carry their own props.
  6. Avoid excessively revealing or offensive attire, to ensure a respectful and enjoyable atmosphere for all.
  7. The participants will be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formation, synchronization, expressions, and costumes, makeup, setup and overall effects.
